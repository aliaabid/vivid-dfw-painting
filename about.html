Who We Are

Built On a Simple Belief

Vivid DFW Painting was founded on a simple belief: homeowners deserve a contractor who communicates clearly, respects their property, and delivers quality work.

We noticed a pattern — homeowners in DFW had been burned by contractors who showed up late, left messes, changed prices mid-project, and disappeared after payment. We built Vivid around being the opposite of that experience.

Every crew member is background-checked, trained in our standards of professionalism, and held accountable to the same level of care we'd expect in our own homes. From the first estimate to the final walkthrough, we make the entire process clear, predictable, and stress-free.

Clear Communication

Respectful of Your Space

No Hidden Costs

On-Time, Every Time

Our Standards

What We Hold Ourselves To

These aren't marketing words — they're the standards our crews are trained on and evaluated against on every single project.

Crew Professionalism

Background-checked teams who show up in uniform, treat your home with care, and work quietly and cleanly throughout the project.

Proactive Communication

You'll never wonder what's happening. We communicate schedule updates, progress, and any questions before they become issues.

Honest Estimates

Every estimate is detailed, itemized, and reviewed by our team. We don't pad prices, add hidden charges, or change scope without approval.

Premium Materials Only

We only apply professional-grade products from Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and BEHR — nothing that will peel, fade, or disappoint.

Respect For Your Home

Floors are covered. Furniture is moved and protected. Landscaping is safeguarded. We leave your home cleaner than we found it.

Satisfaction Walkthrough

Every project ends with a joint walkthrough. If anything isn't exactly right, we fix it before we leave — period.
