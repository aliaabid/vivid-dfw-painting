Professional Cabinet Painters in Dallas

Cabinet painting is one of the highest-ROI home improvements available — it completely transforms the look of your kitchen for a fraction of what new cabinets would cost. But it's also one of the most preparation-intensive painting services, and shortcuts show immediately.

Vivid DFW Painting uses a rigorous multi-step cabinet process that includes full degreasing, sanding, priming with adhesion-bonding products, and spraying (or brush and roll depending on the cabinet style) for a smooth, durable finish that holds up to daily use.

Our Cabinet Painting Process

  • Full removal of cabinet doors and hardware
  • Degreasing — essential for paint adhesion in kitchens
  • Sanding all surfaces to create proper tooth
  • Filling dings, dents, and wood grain as appropriate
  • Application of adhesion-bonding primer
  • Light sanding between coats for ultra-smooth finish
  • Two finish coats — spray or brush depending on cabinet profile
  • Rehang all doors with original or new hardware
  • Final walkthrough and quality inspection

What We Paint

  • Kitchen cabinet doors, frames, and interiors
  • Bathroom vanity cabinets
  • Built-ins, bookcases, and entertainment centers
  • Laundry room cabinetry
  • Cabinet islands and peninsulas
