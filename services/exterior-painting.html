Exterior Painters You Can Trust in DFW

Texas weather is brutal — intense UV, high heat, humidity swings, and storms can peel and fade exterior paint within a few years when the job isn't done right. The difference between a paint job that lasts 3 years and one that lasts 8–10 years is almost entirely preparation and product quality.

At Vivid DFW, we don't cut corners on prep. Every exterior project begins with thorough inspection, cleaning, scraping, caulking, and priming before a single drop of finish coat touches your home's surface.

Our Exterior Process

  • Full exterior inspection and condition assessment
  • Power washing and surface preparation
  • Scraping and removal of all peeling or failing paint
  • Caulking of all gaps, seams, windows, and trim joints
  • Spot priming on bare or repaired surfaces
  • Application of premium exterior finish — 2 coats standard
  • Landscaping and hardscape protection throughout
  • Cleanup and debris removal at project completion
  • Final walkthrough with homeowner

What We Paint

  • Siding (wood, fiber cement, stucco, brick)
  • Fascia boards and soffits
  • Trim, shutters, and decorative accents
  • Front doors and garage doors
  • Fences and gates
  • Decks and covered patios
  • Outbuildings and detached garages

Built for Texas Weather

We exclusively use exterior-grade products formulated for high-UV and high-humidity environments. Our preferred Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore exterior lines are engineered for the DFW climate — superior adhesion, fade resistance, and moisture protection that keeps your home looking fresh for years.
