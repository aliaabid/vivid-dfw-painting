Professional Interior Painters in Dallas

Whether you're freshening up a single bedroom or repainting your entire home from top to bottom, Vivid DFW Painting delivers consistent, professional results in every room we touch.

Our interior painting process starts with proper preparation — the step most painters rush or skip entirely. We protect your flooring and furniture, repair imperfections, prime where needed, and apply your chosen color with attention to every edge, cut line, and finish.

What's Included

  • Complete furniture and floor protection before work begins
  • Surface cleaning, light sanding, and caulking as needed
  • Drywall crack and hole repairs (patching and blending)
  • Ceiling, walls, baseboards, and trim painting
  • Primer application where required for best adhesion
  • Two-coat application for full, even coverage
  • Clean cut lines at ceilings, corners, and trim
  • Full daily cleanup — surfaces and tools
  • Final walkthrough with homeowner before departure

Spaces We Paint

  • Living rooms and family rooms
  • Master bedrooms and guest rooms
  • Kitchens and dining rooms
  • Home offices and studies
  • Hallways, foyers, and stairwells
  • Bathrooms and laundry rooms
  • Ceilings — flat and vaulted
  • Accent walls and feature walls
  • Whole home interior repaints

Paint Products We Use

We work exclusively with professional-grade products from Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and BEHR. These brands offer superior coverage, washability, and color accuracy — your investment looks better and lasts longer.

Not sure which sheen or finish to choose? Our team is happy to walk you through the differences between flat, eggshell, satin, and semi-gloss for each area of your home.
